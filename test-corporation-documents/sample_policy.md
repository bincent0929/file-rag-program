# Acme Corp Internal Policy

## Refund Policy
All purchases are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of the purchase date. 
After 30 days, store credit may be issued at management discretion. 
To initiate a refund, contact support@acme.com with your order number.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Core hours are 10am–3pm local time.
All remote employees must be reachable via Slack during core hours.

## HR Contact
For HR inquiries, contact Sarah Mitchell at hr@acme.com or extension 204.
